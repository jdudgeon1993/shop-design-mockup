Design Lab exists because thoughtfully designed spaces make everyday life more enjoyable. We combine thoughtful design with experienced guidance to create spaces people love to live in — starting with cabinetry, and extending to complete living environments.

Our Story

A better way to buy your dream kitchen.

Buying cabinetry has always meant showroom visits, scheduling around business hours, and a process that moves at someone else's pace. We think there's a better way — and we're not alone. Homeowners are more comfortable than ever purchasing significant pieces for their homes online, and technology now makes it possible to sit down with a real designer at the drop of a hat, from anywhere.

Will video calls ever fully replace the brick-and-mortar showroom? Not completely. But we're challenging that thought — because the process of creating a space you love should be as enjoyable as the space itself.

Design Lab began with three kitchen & bath industry veterans — close to 90 years of collective experience — who share one belief: there's a more enjoyable, more efficient, easier way for people to get the kitchen they've been dreaming about. We're building it.
